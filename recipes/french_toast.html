French Toast

Description

Easy french toast with minimal ingredients

Ingredients

  • 1 Egg
  • Vanilla extract (1 tsp)
  • Cinnamon (1/2 tsp)
  • Milk (1/4 cup)
  • 4 Slices of Bread

Steps

  1. Beat 1 egg, 1 tsp vanilla extract, cinnamon in shallow disk
  2. Stir in 1/4 cup milk
  3. Dip bread in mixture, coat both sides evenly
  4. Cook on lightly greased nonstick griddle on medium heat until both sides brown
