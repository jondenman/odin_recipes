Deviled Eggs

Description

Deviled eggs made to enhance hard boiled eggs

Ingredients

  • 6 Eggs
  • Mayonnaise (3 tablespoons)
  • Mustard (1 teaspoon)
  • Vinegar (1 teaspoon)
  • Salt (1/2 teaspoon)
  • Pepper (1/8 teaspoon)
  • Paprika

Steps

  1. Place eggs gently into saucepan. Add water 1/2'' above eggs. Cover with lid
  2. Heat to boil. Once boiling, turn down heat, cook for 20 minutes
  3. Cool eggs with cold water and peel (leave unpeeled eggs in fridge overnight if possible
  4. Cut Each egg in half lengthwise
  5. Remove yolks with spoon and place all yolks in mixing bowl
  6. Mash yolks with fork
  7. Add 1/2 tsp salt, 1/8 tsp pepper, 1 tsp mustard, 1 tsp vinegar, 3 tsp Mayo. Mix Well
  8. Spoon or pipe mixture into egg whites
  9. Sprinkle Paprika on top
  10. Cover and refrigerate
